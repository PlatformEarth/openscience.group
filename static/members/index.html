OPEN SCIENCE GROUP - Members

Artyom Ruseckiy

Doctorate in Mathematics from the Belarusian State University, the study area is differential equations with stochastic and generalized components. Conducted applied research on mathematical modeling of control systems for unmanned aerial vehicles within the National Academy of Sciences of Belarus. The results of the research were used to build drones. Besides that has more than 8 years of experience in AI systems design and development for world-leading fintech companies such as exp(capital), IG and Capital.com. There he created systems backed by AI technology, algorithms for high-frequency trading and also led product development with more than 50 people in the team. As one of DEIP co-founders, he is contributing to the whole process of the platform elaboration, focusing mainly on the crypto economy model designing.

David Bovill

David is a Founder Member of the Open Science Development Group - working to build a new home for science. Originally qualified in medicine David has worked in multimedia, governance (an originator of Liquid Democracy) and crypto-economics.

Dil Green

Dil is a Founder Member of the Open Science Development Group. A professional architect, Dil is a generalist with a bent for science, complexity theory and economics.

Irina Bolychevsky
